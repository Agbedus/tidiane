Works Authored, Co-authored & Curated

Dr. Ouattara believes in the power of the written word to shape policy, inspire youth, and define Africa's future in space. Below are his publications and a selection of titles he recommends.

Africa & the New Space Race
DR. TIDIANE OUATTARA
Forthcoming — 2025
Africa & the New Space Race
Dr. Ouattara's most ambitious work to date. Drawing on thirty years of experience at the intersection of space science and continental governance, this book argues that Africa's engagement with the new commercial space era is not optional — it is existential. Chapters cover satellite manufacturing ecosystems, spectrum rights negotiations, lunar resource politics, and the economics of building sovereign launch capability.
Publisher: Routledge Africa Studies · Pages: est. 380
Satellites for Sovereignty
OUATTARA & MWANGI — 2022
Published — 2022
Satellites for Sovereignty
A policy framework co-authored with Prof. James Mwangi (University of Nairobi). Essential reading for African government officials and space law practitioners. The book provides a legal and technical blueprint for nations building national space legislation compatible with the Outer Space Treaty while protecting sovereign interests in orbital infrastructure.
Publisher: African University Press · ISBN: 978-9966-XXX-XX-X
Earth Observation for the Sahel
DR. T. OUATTARA — 2018
Published — 2018
Earth Observation for the Sahel
A technical monograph drawing on over a decade of fieldwork across the Sahel. Introduces novel multi-spectral vegetation index methodologies used today in FAO food security monitoring tools. Widely assigned in African geoscience graduate programmes.
Publisher: Springer Nature · DOI: 10.1007/XXXX
